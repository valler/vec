Vectors

Inverse

Negation returns the additive inverse of a vector. Subtraction reverses addition. Scalar division yields the inverse under scalar multiplication, except for zero. Componentwise division also yields the inverse for every vector offset from zero. The last condition expressed in other words: The product of the vector coefficients must be nonzero. the functions are neg, sub, sSub, sDiv, and div.
